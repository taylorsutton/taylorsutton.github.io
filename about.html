About Taylor

Hello! I'm Taylor. I'm a man of many interests. I first started college and was set to be an electrical engineer. I took a lot of gratification in the application of science to solve practical problems. Things took a turn in 2012 when I got turned on to web development and design. I took a few classes to try it out, and I was hooked. You can find samples of my work in the Portfolio page.

My Kata

I take strong influence from Japanese work ethic and design. Aside from the principles of Zen, i believe in maintaining kata or "standards" as a guiding force in life. I believe in creativity as the supreme spirit of design. I believe in honesty as the supreme spirit of living. And I believe in Kaizen as the supreme spirit of conquering problems through persistence.
